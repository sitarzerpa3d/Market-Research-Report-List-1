<p><ul><li>UVA Lights</li><li>UVB Lights</li><li>UVC Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV inspection lights market comprises three primary types: UVA, UVB, and UVC lights. UVA lights, operating at long wavelengths, are mainly used in non-destructive testing and curing applications. UVB lights are less common and primarily used for specific germicidal purposes, offering a balance between UVA and UVC benefits. UVC lights, with short wavelengths, are highly effective for sterilization and disinfection, targeting harmful microorganisms. Each type caters to distinct industrial and medical applications, driving demand across various sectors.</p></p>
