<p><p>The multi-parameter patient monitor market is categorized into high-acuity, mid-acuity, and low-acuity monitors based on patient care levels. High-acuity monitors are used in critical care settings, providing comprehensive vital sign monitoring and advanced functionalities for critically ill patients. Mid-acuity monitors serve patients requiring less intensive observation, often found in telemetry units.
