<p><p>The small unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) market primarily consists of two types: helicopters and multi-rotors. In contrast, multi-rotors, equipped with multiple rotors, excel in maneuverability and simplicity, catering to both recreational and commercial uses like photography and agriculture.
