<p><p>The food coatings market is primarily categorized into dry and liquid types. Dry coatings include powders, seasonings, and breadcrumbs, which provide texture and flavor upon application. They are commonly used for frying or baking, enhancing sensory appeal. Liquid coatings, such as batters and marinades, offer moisture and flavor infusion while allowing for even adhesion and uniform coverage. Both types are essential in food preparation, influencing taste, appearance, and overall consumer experience in various culinary applications.</p></p>
