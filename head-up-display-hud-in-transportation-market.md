<p><ul><li>Windshield-Projected HUDs</li><li>Combiner-Projected HUDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-Up Displays (HUDs) in transportation enhance driver awareness by projecting critical information onto the windshield or a separate combiner screen, allowing drivers to maintain focus on the road. Windshield-Projected HUDs display data directly on the windshield, integrating seamlessly with the vehicle's environment. In contrast, Combiner-Projected HUDs utilize a transparent screen positioned between the driver and the windshield, providing a more compact solution. Both market types contribute to improved safety and navigation in automotive systems, enhancing the driving experience.</p></p>
