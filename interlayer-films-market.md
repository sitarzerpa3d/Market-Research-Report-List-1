<p><p>The interlayer films market comprises several types, primarily EVA, PVB, and SGP films, each serving specific applications in glazing and laminating. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) films offer flexibility and are widely used in consumer electronics and automotive sectors. PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) films provide excellent clarity and are commonly used in architectural glass for safety and sound reduction. SGP (SentryGlas Plus) films are high-strength interlayers ideal for structural and security applications, enhancing durability and impact resistance in various environments.</p></p>
