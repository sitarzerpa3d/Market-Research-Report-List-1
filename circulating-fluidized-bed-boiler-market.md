<p><strong>The Circulating Fluidized Bed Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcritical</li><li>Supercritical</li><li>Ultra-supercritical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) boiler market is categorized into three main types: subcritical, supercritical, and ultra-supercritical. </p><p>Subcritical CFB boilers operate below critical pressure, offering lower efficiency but being cost-effective for smaller capacities. Supercritical CFB boilers function above critical pressure, enhancing thermal efficiency and reducing emissions. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers push the efficiency boundaries even further, operating at higher pressures and temperatures, resulting in minimal fuel consumption and emissions. These advancements cater to increasing demands for cleaner and more efficient energy generation.</p></p>
