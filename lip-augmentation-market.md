<p><ul><li>Temporary Lip Augmentation</li><li>Permanent Lip Augmentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lip augmentation market comprises two primary types: temporary and permanent lip augmentation. Temporary lip augmentation typically involves injectable fillers, such as hyaluronic acid, providing immediate volume that lasts several months. This option appeals to those seeking less commitment and the flexibility to adjust results. In contrast, permanent lip augmentation involves surgical procedures, like implants or fat grafting, offering long-lasting outcomes. Patients opting for permanent solutions prioritize durability and are often more confident in their desired aesthetic changes.</p></p>
