<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic ultrasound systems market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **A-Scan**: Utilizes a one-dimensional ultrasound to measure the length of the eye, aiding in cataract surgery and intraocular lens calculation.</p><p>2. **B-Scan**: Provides two-dimensional cross-sectional images of the eye, useful for diagnosing retinal and other ocular conditions.</p><p>3. **Combined Scan**: Integrates A-Scan and B-Scan capabilities for comprehensive analysis.</p><p>4. **Pachymetry**: Measures corneal thickness, crucial for glaucoma assessment.</p><p>5. **Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)**: Offers high-resolution, cross-sectional images of anterior chamber structures, assisting in diagnosing various eye conditions.</p></p>
