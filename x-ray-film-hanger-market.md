<p><p>The X-ray film hanger market is categorized into two main types: channel type and clip type. Channel type hangers feature a continuous rail or track that allows for easy sliding and organization of films, providing efficient space management. In contrast, clip type hangers use clips to secure individual X-ray films, offering versatility in film handling and display. Both types are designed to ensure proper drying, storage, and accessibility of X-ray films in medical and diagnostic environments.</p></p>
