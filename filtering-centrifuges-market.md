<p><ul><li>Horizontal Centrifuges</li><li>Vertical Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filtering centrifuges market comprises horizontal and vertical centrifuges, each suited for different applications. Horizontal centrifuges utilize a cylindrical bowl that rotates horizontally, effectively separating solids from liquids in large volumes, making them ideal for continuous processes. Vertical centrifuges, on the other hand, have a vertical orientation, facilitating easier discharge of sediment and are commonly used for fine particle separation. Both types are essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing, focusing on efficiency and product purity.</p></p>
