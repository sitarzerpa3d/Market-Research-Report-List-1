<p><p>The body fat measurement market encompasses various technologies to assess body composition. Bioimpedance Analyzers (BIA) use electrical currents to estimate fat percentage. Skinfold Calipers measure skinfold thickness at specific sites for fat estimation. Hydrostatic Weighing (HwD) calculates body density underwater for accurate fat analysis. Air Displacement Plethysmography (ADP) measures body volume and density via air displacement. Dual Emission X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) offers precise data on bone density and fat distribution using X-ray technology, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
