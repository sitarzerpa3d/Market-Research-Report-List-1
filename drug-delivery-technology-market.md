<p><p>The drug delivery technology market encompasses various methods for administering medications effectively. Oral delivery is the most common, involving tablets or liquids swallowed for systemic absorption. Injectable methods, including syringes and pens, allow for precise dosing. Topical delivery applies drugs directly to the skin. Ocular methods target eye conditions, while nasal delivery uses the nasal cavity for rapid absorption. Pulmonary delivery involves inhalation for respiratory diseases. Transmucosal methods use mucous membranes for quick absorption, and implantable devices release medication over time within the body.</p></p>
