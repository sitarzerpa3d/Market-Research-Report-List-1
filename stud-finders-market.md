<p><p>The stud finders market is primarily divided into two types: magnetic and electronic stud finders. Magnetic stud finders detect metal fasteners, such as nails or screws, embedded in walls, making them simple and cost-effective. In contrast, electronic stud finders use sensors to identify changes in density and can locate both wood and metal studs, offering enhanced accuracy and additional features like depth detection. Each type caters to different user needs, from DIY homeowners to professional contractors.</p></p>
