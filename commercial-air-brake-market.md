<p><ul><li>Air Disc Brake</li><li>Air Drum Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial air brake market is primarily divided into two types: air disc brakes and air drum brakes. Air disc brakes utilize a rotor and caliper design for efficient braking performance, offering superior heat dissipation and shorter stopping distances, making them popular in modern heavy vehicles. In contrast, air drum brakes feature a drum and shoe mechanism, providing reliable performance and cost-effectiveness, historically favored in commercial applications. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring vehicle safety and reliability.</p></p>
