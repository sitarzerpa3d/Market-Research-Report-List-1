<p><p>The stainless steel gratings market comprises various types, including welded, swage locked, press locked, and others. Welded grating features a robust construction with intersecting bars fused together, ideal for heavy loads. Swage locked grating involves a distinctive locking mechanism, ensuring durability and ease of installation. Press locked grating uses a pressing method to secure the crossbars tightly, providing strength and stability. Other types may include extruded and fabricated designs, catering to specific applications and environment needs.</p></p>
