<p><p>The Control Moment Gyroscope (CMG) market comprises two main types: single-gimbal and dual-gimbal systems. Single-gimbal CMGs utilize one gimbal to control the rotation of a spinning wheel, offering simplicity and efficiency for spacecraft attitude control. In contrast, dual-gimbal CMGs feature two gimbals, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and precision by independently adjusting the orientation of the spinning wheel. Both types play critical roles in stabilizing and controlling the orientation of various aerospace applications, such as satellites and spacecraft.</p></p>
