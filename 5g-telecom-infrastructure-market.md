<p><p>The 5G telecom infrastructure market features several cell types that enhance connectivity. Femtocells are small, low-power cellular base stations typically used in homes or small businesses to improve indoor coverage. Picocells are larger than femtocells and are suited for public venues like shopping centers, providing coverage to a moderate number of users. Microcells cover larger areas than picocells and are often deployed in urban environments to boost network capacity. Together, these solutions optimize coverage and capacity for diverse user demands.</p></p>
