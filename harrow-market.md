<p><p>Harrow market types are essential for soil preparation in agriculture, each serving distinct purposes. Spring harrows use flexible tines for leveling and breaking up soil clumps. Roller harrows compact soil while smoothing the surface. Chain harrows consist of interlinked chains that break up soil and incorporate debris. Disc harrows employ rotating discs to cut and mix soil effectively. Additionally, other types may include variations like combination harrows or specialized models, catering to specific farming needs and enhancing cultivation efficiency.</p></p>
