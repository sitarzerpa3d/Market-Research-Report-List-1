<p><p>The impact hammer market is primarily divided into hydraulic and electric types. Hydraulic impact hammers utilize fluid pressure to deliver high-energy blows, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications like driving piles in construction and infrastructure projects. In contrast, electric impact hammers harness electrical energy to deliver rapid, powerful impacts, offering increased agility and precision in lighter applications, such as demolition and renovation. Both types enhance efficiency and productivity, catering to various industry needs and project scales.</p></p>
