<p><ul><li>Rail Side Installation</li><li>On-rail Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Axle Counter System for rail transit can be categorized into two main types: Rail Side Installation and On-Rail Installation. Rail Side Installation involves placing the axle counter equipment alongside the tracks, where sensors detect the passage of trains through tracking points. In contrast, On-Rail Installation places the equipment directly onto the trains themselves. This allows for real-time monitoring and data collection on axle movement and train position, enhancing safety and operational efficiency in rail transit systems.</p></p>
