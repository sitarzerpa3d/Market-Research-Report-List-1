<p><p>The liquid seaweed derivatives market comprises three primary types based on seaweed color: red, brown, and green. Red seaweed, rich in carrageenan, is utilized in food and cosmetic applications. Brown seaweed, often high in alginate, finds use in the food industry, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture as a thickening and gelling agent. Green seaweed, known for its nutritional benefits, is mainly used in dietary supplements and functional foods. Each type offers unique properties suited for various industrial uses.</p></p>
