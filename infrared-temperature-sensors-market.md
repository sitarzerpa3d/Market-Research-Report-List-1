<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared temperature sensors market is segmented into two main types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors measure temperature by directly touching the target surface, offering high precision for applications that allow physical contact. Non-contact type sensors, on the other hand, detect infrared radiation emitted from objects without physical interaction, making them ideal for measuring the temperature of moving or inaccessible targets. Both types are widely used in various industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>
