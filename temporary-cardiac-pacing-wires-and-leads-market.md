<p><p>Temporary cardiac pacing wires and leads are essential for managing patients with transient cardiac rhythm disturbances. The market is primarily categorized into unipolar and bipolar types. Unipolar leads utilize a single electrode for pacing and a large indifferent electrode, offering simplicity and less tissue irritation. Bipolar leads feature two closely spaced electrodes, providing more localized stimulation and reducing the risk of diaphragmatic stimulation. Other types may include specialized leads for unique clinical scenarios, enhancing versatility in temporary cardiac pacing.</p></p>
