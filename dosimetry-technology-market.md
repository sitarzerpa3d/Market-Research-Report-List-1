<p><p>The dosimetry technology market encompasses various types of radiation detection methods, including Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescent Dosimeters (OSL), and Radio Photoluminescent Dosimeters (RPL). TLDs measure ionizing radiation by analyzing light emitted from heated materials, while OSLs utilize light stimulation to assess radiation exposure. RPLs employ a similar concept but rely on luminescent materials that emit light upon exposure to radiation. Each type offers unique advantages in precision, sensitivity, and application across industries such as healthcare, nuclear, and research.</p></p>
