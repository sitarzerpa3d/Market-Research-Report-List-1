<p><p>The fruit pomace market is categorized into three primary types: powder, pellets, and others. Fruit pomace powder is created by drying and grinding the residual pulp, seeds, and skin, making it suitable for use in dietary supplements, functional foods, and natural colorants. Pellets, formed by compressing pomace, are primarily utilized as animal feed and in biofuel production due to their high fiber content. The "others" category includes various forms, such as extracts and juices, catering to niche applications in culinary and industrial sectors.</p></p>
