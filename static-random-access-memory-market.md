<p><p>The Static Random Access Memory (SRAM) market encompasses several segments based on performance and power consumption. NVSRAM (Non-Volatile SRAM) retains data without power, ideal for backup applications. Asynchronous SRAM offers fast access times without needing a clock signal, suitable for low-latency applications. Synchronous SRAM synchronizes with a clock, enhancing speed and efficiency, making it ideal for high-performance tasks. Low Power SRAM is designed for energy efficiency, commonly used in mobile and embedded devices, balancing performance with power conservation.</p></p>
