<p><p>The dome security camera market comprises primarily two types: analog cameras and IP cameras. Analog cameras utilize traditional coaxial cables for video transmission, offering basic surveillance capabilities and lower costs, making them suitable for smaller installations. In contrast, IP cameras use network connections for higher-resolution video and advanced features such as remote access and integration with smart systems. IP cameras are increasingly preferred for their scalability, flexibility, and superior image quality, catering to diverse security needs in various environments.</p></p>
