<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mask Aligner</li><li>Laser Direct Imaging</li><li>Projection</li><li>Laser Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithography equipment market encompasses several technologies: </p><p>1. **Mask Aligner** - Utilizes masks to project patterns onto substrates for precise alignment, commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing. </p><p>2. **Laser Direct Imaging** - Employs lasers to directly transfer images onto surfaces, allowing for high-resolution patterning without masks. </p><p>3. **Projection Lithography** - Involves using optical systems to project images of masks onto wafers, suitable for large-scale production. </p><p>4. **Laser Ablation** - Employs lasers to remove material from a substrate, enabling precise fabrication of microstructures. Each type caters to specific application needs in electronics and photonics.</p></p>
