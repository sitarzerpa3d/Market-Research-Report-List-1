<p><p>The 12-inch wafer dicing machine market includes two main types: grinding wheel dicing machines and laser dicing machines. Grinding wheel dicing machines utilize abrasive wheels to cut wafers, offering precision and cost-effectiveness for various materials. In contrast, laser dicing machines employ focused laser beams to achieve high-speed and flexible dicing with minimal mechanical stress, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for efficient wafer processing in the electronics industry.</p></p>
